Correct Answers:

Workplace Hazard Possible Risk Safety Measure

2. Dust and Debris Respiratory issues Use protective masks and clean work area
3. Crimping RJ45 connectors incorrectly Poor network connection Proper crimping tools and technique
4. Cluttered tools and materials Accidents and injuries Maintain organized workstations
5. Testing live cable networks Electric shock Turn off power before testing
