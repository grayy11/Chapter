Introduction

In our last few lessons, we covered a lot, from what computer networks are to how IP addresses work and subnetting. If you’ve been keeping up, awesome! If not, no worries, we’ll build on that foundation today.

  • IP Addressing (L2.2): Learned how IPs work as unique identifiers.
